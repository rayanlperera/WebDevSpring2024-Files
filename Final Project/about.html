About Me

Hi there my name is Rayan Perera and I'm a junior at NYU studying Integrated Design Media. Record Scratch was created just simply because I love music and always wanted to pretend I had my own vinyl collection. I've wanted to have a vinyl collection to play music for myself or friends and this is my way of doing it! Here what you'll expect to see is some of the albums that I personally love and my opinions on them. Whether you should listen to them or not. But that's not all. Sure this is my own collection but I'm opening to hearing out your opinions! In the contact tab you can message and tell me what album I should add to my collection. Just fill out the form and hey, maybe your album will be next in my collection! Hope you enjoy!
